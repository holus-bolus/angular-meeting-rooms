Instruction

One to one is a periodic conversation with an employee conducted in a free form to discuss all current questions, brilliant ideas or disappointments

Before the meeting:

– define and convey an agenda (a list of topics for discussion at One to one)

– gather information (get feedback in advance, check goals, etc.)

At the meeting:

– define the context and structure of a meeting

– start with a small talk (casual conversation)

– it is important to conduct a conversation informally

– give and receive feedback with an interlocutor

– define a list of actions based on the conversation

After the meeting:

– make a follow up (the result of the conversation)

– check out after the conversation

– give feedback on the result of the conversation (both positive and negative)

– add information about the conducted One to one (Editing and deleting One to one is possible within 7 days after entering the data)
